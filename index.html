---------------------------------------
ABOUT
---------------------------------------

カイヴェーン Studio is a tiny as in solo studio. Games, stories, and that moment you whisper, "wait... is this
idea too weird?"

I do not chase trends. I chase that specific thing: when a game makes
you laugh, then think, then cry, then care about a character made of 12 polygons
and one sound effect like it is your oldest friend.

Built for:
  - narrative-heavy games
  - experimental mechanics
  - remote-first dev
  - indie at heart
  - story + mechanics = heart
